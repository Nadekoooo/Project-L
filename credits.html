Roles and Their Meanings

  • Paladin: A symbol of unwavering faith and protection.
  • Assassin: Precision, strategy, and the quiet resolve to act in shadows.
  • Warrior: Commitment to strength, leadership, and courage in battle.
  • Mage: Wisdom, creativity, and the power of knowledge.
  • Cleric: Compassion, healing, and the light of hope.
  • Archer: Patience, accuracy, and harmony with nature.
  • Bard: Inspiration, storytelling, and the unity of shared experiences.
